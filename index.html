Everything you need

Built for how operators actually work

Every feature was designed around real utility operations — not generic software retrofitted for water.

Chlorine Residual Tracking
Log readings across all sample points with real-time dosing guidance. Color-coded compliance status keeps you ahead of permit limits.
Compliance Calendar
Never miss a deadline. Track sampling schedules, reporting due dates, and regulatory events automatically.
Daily Rounds Checklist
GPS-verified rounds with shift reset. Document every inspection with timestamps and keep your records audit-ready.
DLQOR & DMR Reports
Auto-calculate quarterly and monthly discharge reports from your logged data. Export ready-to-submit reports in minutes.
Real-Time Compliance Alerts
Instant alerts when readings fall outside permit limits. Sidebar badges keep critical issues visible at all times.
Multi-Site & Team Support
Manage multiple facilities under one account. Invite operators, assign roles, and keep your whole team on the same page.
